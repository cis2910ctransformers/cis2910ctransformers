Developments

There are many developments that heavily uses Machine Learning. A great example of this is Voice Assistantance. One company that I believe stands out the most is Google. Google Voice Assistance is so brilliant and understands its mistakes that it hardly fails. Google has put in many investments and effort to prefect it's own software AI that I believe it beats its competitors like Apple Siri, Amazon Alexa, and Windows Cortana but none the less all are heavily being used by Machine-Learning and learning under these developments.

Another great development in machine-learning that has been floating around a lot more as well is facial recognition. A lot of this comes from cell phones detecting real-time facial recognition as additional security and added level of biometrics. Some examples of today world using facial recognition are

  1. Major League Baseball will soon start allow fans to validate tickets by scanning their face.
  2. Singapore's new mega-mall will track shoppers and recommend deals to them, on the fly.
  3. The organizing committee behind the 2020 Olympics in Tokoyo has said it will include facial recognition systems in order to improve security.

Accomplishments

One of the most greastest accomplishments that was made by the evolution of Machine-Learning technology was when AI defeated a professional E-Sport team. A project naming themselves OpenAI Five created a video game bot to play against human players in a competitive five-on-five video game called Dota 2. Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA), which could take months and even years to master and prefect in the understanding of the game. These AI bots were able to use machine-learning to adapt and learn as they play the game to defeat the human players in a 24 minute game.
